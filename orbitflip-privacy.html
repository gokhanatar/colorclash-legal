Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 4, 2026

OrbitFlip ("we", "our", or "the app") is an arcade game developed for iOS. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use OrbitFlip.

1. Information We Collect

OrbitFlip does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information directly. We do not require account creation, login, or any personal data to use the app.

The app stores your preferences (language, selected mode) and high scores locally on your device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device.

2. Third-Party Services

OrbitFlip integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Google AdMob — We display banner advertisements to non-premium users. Google AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Game Center — Used for leaderboards. Game Center is managed by Apple and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit — Used for in-app purchases (subscriptions and one-time purchases). All payment processing is handled by Apple.

3. In-App Purchases

OrbitFlip offers optional premium subscriptions (monthly and yearly) and a one-time lifetime purchase. All purchases are processed through the Apple App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

  • Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your device Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.

4. Children's Privacy

OrbitFlip includes a "Kids Mode" designed for younger players. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information from any user. When Kids Mode is active, advertisements may still be shown via AdMob, which complies with COPPA through Google's child-directed treatment settings.

5. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no data to retain or delete. Local preferences and scores can be cleared by deleting the app from your device.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
