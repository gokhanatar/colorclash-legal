Privacy Policy

Introduction

Muslim ("we", "our", or "the App") is a comprehensive Islamic companion application developed for iOS and Android platforms. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how your data is handled. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

1. Location Data

We request access to your device's location services to provide:

  • Prayer Times: Accurate calculation of daily prayer times based on your geographic coordinates
  • Qibla Direction: Determining the direction of the Kaaba in Mecca from your current location
  • Ramadan Schedule: Calculating Imsak (pre-dawn) and Iftar (sunset) times during Ramadan

Location data is processed locally on your device. We do not transmit, store, or share your location data with any third parties or remote servers.

2. Local Storage Data

The App stores the following data locally on your device using standard browser/app storage mechanisms:

  • Prayer time settings and calculation method preferences
  • Favorite Quran verses and personal notes
  • Quran reading progress, streaks, and daily goals
  • Tasbih (digital counter) counts and custom dhikr entries
  • Personal duas (prayers) created by the user
  • Ramadan fasting tracker data
  • Notification and alarm preferences
  • Theme and language preferences
  • Widget configuration data

All user data is stored exclusively on your device. We do not have access to, collect, or transmit any of this data to external servers.

3. Advertising Data

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements in the free version. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising identifier (GAID on Android, IDFA on iOS)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • IP address (for geographic ad targeting)
  • App interaction data (ad views, clicks)

For more information about Google's data practices, please visit: Google Privacy Policy.

4. Purchase Data

In-app purchases (Pro subscription) are processed entirely through Google Play Store (Android) or Apple App Store (iOS). We do not collect, process, or store any payment information. All billing is handled by the respective platform's payment system.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide accurate prayer times and Qibla direction
  • To maintain your reading progress and personal preferences
  • To deliver timely prayer notifications and Islamic event reminders
  • To display relevant advertisements (free version only)
  • To process subscription purchases through platform stores

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. The only third-party service integrated into the App is Google AdMob for advertising purposes, which operates under its own privacy policy.

Data Security

Since all user data is stored locally on your device, data security is maintained through your device's built-in security features (screen lock, encryption). We recommend keeping your device's operating system updated for optimal security.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages as a religious education and practice tool. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App does not require account creation or personal information input beyond local device storage.

Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all App data at any time by:

  • Clearing the App's data through your device settings
  • Uninstalling the App

Upon uninstallation, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored by the App (viewable within the App itself)
  • Delete all data at any time (via device settings or uninstallation)
  • Opt out of personalized advertising (via device ad settings)
  • Disable location services (prayer times can be set manually)
  • Disable notifications at any time
