Introduction

ColorClash ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Do Not Collect

ColorClash is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not require account registration
  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your photos, camera, or microphone
  • We do not share any data with third parties for tracking purposes

Data Stored Locally

ColorClash stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game progress and scores
  • Theme and settings preferences
  • Achievement and badge status
  • Purchase status (Premium subscription, credits)
  • Sound preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

In-App Purchases

ColorClash offers optional in-app purchases (Premium subscriptions and credit packs) through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information. Purchase history is managed by Apple's systems.

Advertising

ColorClash displays advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect certain device information and advertising identifiers to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's privacy settings. Premium subscribers enjoy an ad-free experience.

For more information about Google's data practices, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

Game Center

ColorClash integrates with Apple Game Center for leaderboards and achievements. Game Center data is managed by Apple according to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your Game Center personal information.

Children's Privacy

ColorClash includes a parental gate (math verification) before any in-app purchase screens. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to play.

Data Security

Since all game data is stored locally on your device and we do not collect or transmit personal information, there is no risk of data breach from our servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
